About This Event:

Are you a homeowner, a San Diego real estate investor, or an agent needing to maximize ADU opportunities for your clients? Join us to learn about building Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs) in San Diego.

What Are ADUs?

ADUs, or Accessory Dwelling Units, are secondary housing units built on residential properties. They offer a versatile and cost-effective solution for creating extra space, generating rental income, or enhancing property value.

Why Should You Attend?
